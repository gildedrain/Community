Become a Notary Public in NYS

This course will cover information to prepare you for the New York State Notary exam. Taught by an experienced practicing professional, the course will review the laws and procedures specific to the field in easy-to-understand language and include information on best practices as well as tips on how you can make a full-time or part-time career out of being a Notary Public.

Title: Become Notary Public
